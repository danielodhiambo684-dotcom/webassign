Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a rental?

You can apply directly through our website after creating an account. Each property listing has an "Apply Now" button.

Are there any application fees?

No. RentHub never charges tenants application or processing fees.

How are properties verified?

All listings are verified by our team through direct communication with landlords and on-site checks.

Can I schedule a tour?

Yes! Click "Schedule Tour" on any property page to choose a date and time.
